# Climate & Health Intelligence Platform

AI-powered platform for monitoring environmental risks and improving public health outcomes through data-driven insights.

## Overview
The Climate & Health Intelligence Platform is a data-driven solution designed to monitor, analyze, and communicate environmental risks that impact public health, especially for vulnerable communities and children.

## Problem
Communities in Kosovo face increasing environmental risks such as air pollution, illegal waste dumping, and climate-related hazards. However, there is a lack of real-time data and early warning systems that connect environmental issues with public health outcomes.

## Solution
Our platform aggregates community-reported data, environmental indicators, and geospatial inputs to identify pollution hotspots and predict potential health risks. Using AI and data science, the system transforms raw data into actionable insights that support early warning and decision-making.

## Key Features
- Community reporting of environmental hazards  
- AI-based risk detection and pattern recognition  
- Visualization of pollution hotspots  
- Early warning alerts for high-risk areas  

## Technology
- AI / Data Science  
- Geospatial analysis  
- Open data integration  

## Status
Prototype phase with initial validation using community-generated data.

## Open Source Commitment
This project is being developed as an open-source solution to ensure transparency, collaboration, and scalability.
